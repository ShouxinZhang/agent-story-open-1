# Chapter 3 — Emerald Breeze

<font color="#808080">**Description:** The next morning. Sunlight filtered through the canopy of hundred-meter-tall trees, casting dappled golden coins across the forward operating base. The air was warm, thick with oxygen and the faint sweetness of alien pollen. After a night of cultivation — during which Xin had quietly pushed his Longevity Art to Layer 2 of the Qi Refining stage — the base felt almost peaceful.</font>

<font color="#808080">**Description:** Near the motor pool, three standard Federation hover patrol vehicles sat charging on their magnetic pads. They resembled elongated speeders — open-top, two-row seating, with a low-slung repulsor array and a retractable sensor mast. Designed for coastal island reconnaissance, not combat. Perfect for a morning familiarization run.</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (tossing a keycard in the air and catching it) "The liaison officer said we should survey the northern shoreline before our first assignment. I requisitioned a patrol speeder. Who's driving?"</font>

<font color="#6CB4EE">**Su Wanshuang:** (arms folded, leaning against a supply crate) "You requisitioned it, you drive."</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (grinning, spinning the keycard toward Xin) "Nah. The youngest member does the driving. It's tradition."</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** (catching the card with one hand) "Pretty sure you just invented that tradition three seconds ago."</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (already climbing into the back seat) "Traditions have to start somewhere."</font>

<font color="#808080">**Description:** Xin slid into the driver's seat and scanned the console. The controls were intuitive — throttle on the right grip, pitch on the left, holographic HUD projected onto the windshield rail. At fifteen, he was the youngest special-recruit in the entire Joint Training program: a cybersecurity and information-warfare prodigy fast-tracked through Dragon Nation's national examination system. His admission file listed him as an "IT-class exceptional entrant." Most of the Federation soldiers at base hadn't believed the number when they checked his file.</font>

<font color="#808080">**Description:** He tapped the ignition. The speeder hummed to life, lifting half a meter off the ground on a cushion of pale blue repulsor light. Su Wanshuang took the front passenger seat without comment.</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (leaning forward between the two front seats) "For the record, I ran a background check on you last night."</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** (steering the speeder onto the jungle trail toward the coast) "Find anything interesting?"</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** "Fifteen years old. One-seventy-six. Awakened B-rank Lightning Elementalization at thirteen — two years ahead of the national median. Entered the university on a special IT recruitment track at fourteen. Also —" (she tilted her head) "— your Academy ID photo is unreasonably pretty. A few girls in the database forum actually asked if you were filed under the wrong gender."</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** (expression unchanged, eyes on the trail) "I'm going to take that as a compliment and move on with my life."</font>

<font color="#6CB4EE">**Su Wanshuang:** (very quietly, almost to herself) "It wasn't inaccurate."</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (delighted gasp) "Wanshuang! Did you just agree he's pretty?"</font>

<font color="#6CB4EE">**Su Wanshuang:** (turning to look out at the passing jungle, voice flat) "I stated a factual observation about bone structure. Don't extrapolate."</font>

<font color="#808080">**Description:** The jungle thinned as they reached the coast. Xin guided the speeder over a low ridge of volcanic rock, and suddenly, the full panorama of the northern shore unfolded before them — turquoise water stretching to a horizon that curved noticeably more than Earth's, dotted with dozens of smaller islands wrapped in emerald green. Under Neptune Star's binary moons — both still visible as pale crescents in the morning sky — the ocean glittered like shattered glass.</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (standing up in the back seat, wind whipping her black hair) "Okay. I take back every complaint about this deployment. This is gorgeous."</font>

<font color="#808080">**Description:** Xin banked the speeder left, following the shoreline at a comfortable forty kilometers per hour. The repulsors kicked up a fine mist of spray behind them. The 1.18G gravity made the ride feel a touch heavier than it would on Earth, the speeder settling a few centimeters lower on hard turns, but the coastal breeze was warm and the air smelled of salt and alien jasmine.</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** "So — two A-ranks from major Federation families, and one B-rank IT kid. Any theories on why they put us in the same squad?"</font>

<font color="#6CB4EE">**Su Wanshuang:** "Standard tactical doctrine. Two frontline combatants, one technical support. Your gene rank is irrelevant; your information warfare certification is what they wanted."</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** "Glad to know I'm valued for my personality."</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (laughing) "Your personality is fine. Very dry. Very fifteen-year-old-pretending-to-be-twenty."</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** "I don't pretend. I just skip the unnecessary parts of conversation."</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** "See? That's exactly what a twenty-year-old would say." (she leaned over and poked his shoulder) "Relax a little. We're not on mission yet. Tell us something fun. Hobbies? Embarrassing childhood stories? Secret celebrity crush?"</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** (slight pause) "I once wrote a worm that accidentally propagated through my middle school's entire network and made every screen display a dancing cat for six hours."</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (eyes wide) "You're joking."</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** "The principal didn't think so."</font>

<font color="#6CB4EE">**Su Wanshuang:** (the faintest twitch at the corner of her mouth) "And they still gave you a special recruitment offer after that?"</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** "The Federation cybersecurity division saw the incident report. Apparently, writing a self-replicating polymorphic program at twelve is more impressive than it is illegal. They offered the recruitment slot the same week."</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (clapping slowly) "So you're telling me our tech support got into university because of a dancing-cat virus. This is the best squad backstory I've ever heard."</font>

<font color="#808080">**Description:** They rounded a headland and passed over a shallow lagoon where a pod of alien creatures — something like translucent manta rays, each five meters across — glided beneath the surface. Their bioluminescent patterns pulsed in slow, rhythmic waves of pale green and violet.</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (leaning over the edge) "Oh — those are Emerald Gliders. F-rank, completely harmless. The locals use them for underwater transport."</font>

<font color="#6CB4EE">**Su Wanshuang:** (watching the creatures with her pale-gold eyes, voice softer than usual) "Their bio-electric field is… calming. Like a low-frequency resonance."</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** (glancing at her) "Your Frost Domain lets you sense that?"</font>

<font color="#6CB4EE">**Su Wanshuang:** "Thermal perception. Their bodies are two degrees colder than the surrounding water. The pattern is remarkably regular." (pause) "It is… pleasant."</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (stage whisper to Xin) "That's Su Wanshuang for 'I think they're cute.'"</font>

<font color="#6CB4EE">**Su Wanshuang:** "I can freeze your vocal cords from here, Lingshan."</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (grinning, not even slightly intimidated) "Worth it."</font>

<font color="#808080">**Description:** Xin allowed himself a small smile. For a moment, gliding along the shimmering coast of an alien ocean with the warm wind in his hair, it was easy to forget the system warning still blinking quietly in the back of his consciousness. Easy to forget the S-rank energy signature in the deep southern trench. Easy to forget that he was a displaced soul piloting a stolen life through a universe that ran on rules he was still learning.</font>

<font color="#808080">**Description:** The speeder crested another rise, and ahead, a cluster of indigenous structures came into view — conical dwellings built from massive shells and coral, arranged around a central plaza where Neptune Star's native humanoids were going about their morning routines. They were tall, blue-skinned, with fin-like ridges along their forearms and necks, and they paused to watch the Federation speeder pass with expressions of cautious curiosity.</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** "Local settlement. Should we stop and say hello?"</font>

<font color="#6CB4EE">**Su Wanshuang:** "Negative. Unauthorized contact with indigenous populations requires a liaison officer present. We observe and report."</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** (waving at a group of children who had gathered at the village edge, their large, dark eyes blinking) "Relax, I'm just waving. Diplomacy starts with good vibes." (the children waved back, one of them flashing a toothy grin) "See? Universal language."</font>

<font color="#808080">**Description:** They continued north for another twenty minutes, cataloging terrain features, potential landing zones, and freshwater sources on the speeder's mapping system. Xin handled the driving with quiet precision, his long fingers light on the controls — a detail Ye Lingshan did not fail to notice.</font>

<font color="#F5A623">**Ye Lingshan:** "You know, for a B-rank lightning user, you're weirdly graceful. Most lightning-types I've met are all twitchy reflexes and zero finesse."</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** "Lightning is just high-speed information transfer. Precision matters more than power."</font>

<font color="#6CB4EE">**Su Wanshuang:** (turning to look at him with something that might have been interest) "That's an unusual interpretation."</font>

<font color="#4A90D9">**Xin:** "IT kid, remember? Everything is data."</font>

<font color="#808080">**Description:** The speeder banked in a wide arc and began heading back toward base. Behind them, the twin moons were sinking toward the western ocean, and the morning light had turned the water from turquoise to deep, molten gold. Somewhere far to the south, beneath kilometers of alien ocean, something ancient shifted in its sleep — but here, on the northern shore, the breeze was warm, the company was good, and for one quiet morning, that was enough.</font>
