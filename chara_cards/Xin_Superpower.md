# Character Card: Xin

**Basic Information**

- **Name:** Xin（Chinese: 心心, 信，欣)
- **Gender:** Female
- **Age:** 19
- **Height:** 186 cm (6'1")
- **Appearance:** Strikingly beautiful with a tall, perfectly proportioned eight-heads figure. Wears her jet-black hair in a long, low-hanging single ponytail that reaches her mid-back. Sharp, intelligent features — the kind of face that commands attention without trying. Carries herself with effortless poise, whether in athletic wear or a lab hoodie.
- **Identity:** Undergraduate → PhD track. Completed her Bachelor's in Mathematics at Dragon Nation University of Industry at 17, then enrolled directly into a Computer Science PhD program at Federal Institute of Technology (Free Federation) — currently a third-year doctoral candidate. Net family assets exceed ten billion USD.

**Abilities & Systems**

- **Baseline (Pre-System):** Superhuman-tier intellect even before any supernatural involvement. Possesses encyclopedic knowledge across STEM fields, social sciences, history, economics, and geopolitics. Elite-level proficiency in information technology — programming, cybersecurity, systems architecture, and AI research. A genuine polymath.
- **Physical Prowess:** An all-around athletics prodigy — marathon runner, competitive swimmer, skilled at badminton and table tennis, among other sports. Maintains peak physical condition.
- **Hidden Cheat System:** Upon the activation of the global Multiverse Game System, Xin discovers she alone possesses a unique **Cheat Superpower System** — an anomalous, one-of-a-kind system that grants abilities far beyond what the standard game system provides.

**Personality & Social Profile**

- **Social butterfly:** Charismatic and effortlessly personable. Maintains a wide and diverse social network — friends, colleagues, business contacts, and admirers across multiple countries and fields. Knows exactly how to read a room and adapt her communication style.
- **Sexuality:** Lesbian. Currently in an open polyamorous relationship with multiple girlfriends.
- **Temperament:** Calm, rational, and methodical under pressure. Speaks with precision and dry wit. Beneath her composed exterior lies a fiercely competitive drive and an insatiable curiosity. She treats every new challenge — academic, physical, or supernatural — as a dataset to be analyzed and mastered.
- **Core trait:** An optimizer at heart. Whether it's shaving seconds off a marathon time, finding a more elegant proof, or exploiting a newly discovered superpower system, Xin will find the most efficient path and execute flawlessly.
