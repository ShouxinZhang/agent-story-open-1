# Gene Entry

## Title
Solar War Engine

## Rank
S

## Category
Body Supersystem / Stellar Thermal Conversion

## Overview
Solar War Engine is an S-rank assault bloodline that converts heat, radiation, and metabolic reserves into a star-forged battle state. In Interstellar Federation doctrine, the gene is treated as a siege-class breakthrough asset because a single mature carrier can survive heavy weapon fire, breach fortified alloy, and keep fighting after damage that would kill lesser awakened soldiers.

## Signature Traits
- Builds a radiant exoshell that amplifies strength, durability, and impact force.
- Converts ambient heat and directed energy into short-term combat reserves.
- Vents solar plasma through fists, shoulders, or spinal channels during peak output.
- Sustains emergency regeneration by burning stored thermal energy through damaged tissue.

## Combat Use
Solar War Engine is deployed in breach assaults, monster extermination, reactor-zone warfare, and high-pressure boarding actions. A trained bearer can open corridors through fortress defenses, anchor a frontline under orbital bombardment, and overpower heavily armored targets through repeated thermal shock impacts.

## Limitations
- Peak activation consumes oxygen, nutrients, and trace minerals at extreme rates.
- Vacuum-cold environments reduce efficiency unless external heat capture is available.
- Fine motor control and tactical precision drop during full overclock states.
- Failed vent cycles can cook nerves, lungs, or internal organs from within.

## Awakening Cues
- Ember-red circuit lines appear under the skin after intense exertion or injury.
- Core body temperature rises far above human norms without immediate collapse.
- Muscles produce visible heat haze during training or emotional spikes.
- Medical scans show impossible caloric loss and unusual radiant output near the sternum.

## Common Progression
- Lv.1: The user manifests localized heat reinforcement and unstable burst strikes.
- Lv.2: Thermal reserves can be stored and released through repeated armored charges.
- Lv.3: The exoshell stabilizes enough for sustained battlefield deployment.
- Lv.4: Plasma venting and emergency regeneration become reliable under heavy fire.
- Lv.5: The bearer functions as a mobile siege engine whose body behaves like a controlled furnace core.
